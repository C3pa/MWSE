# AI Behavior States

!!! tip
	These values are available in Lua by their identifier in the `tes3.aiBehaviorState.*` namespace. For example, `tes3.aiBehaviorState.walk` has a value of `8`.

ID  | Behavior State
--- | --------------
-1  | Undecided
0   | Hello
2   | Idle
3   | Attack
4	| Avoid
6   | Flee
8   | Walk
12  | Greet
