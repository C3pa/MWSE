# Dialogue Pages

!!! tip
	These values are available in Lua by their identifier in the `tes3.dialoguePage.voice.*` namespace. For example, `tes3.dialoguePage.voice.attack` has a value of `5`.

ID | Voice
-- | -----------
0  | Hello
1  | Idle
2  | Intruder
3  | Thief
4  | Hit
5  | Attack
6  | Flee
