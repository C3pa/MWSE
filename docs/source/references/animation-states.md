# Animation States

!!! tip
	These values are available in Lua by their identifier in the `tes3.animationState.*` namespace. For example, `tes3.animationState.swingHit` has a value of `4`.

ID  | State
--- | ----------------------
0   | Idle
1   | Ready
2   | Swing Up
3   | Swing Down
4   | Swing Hit
5   | Swing Follow Light
6   | Swing Follow Medium
7   | Swing Follow Heavy
8   | Readying Weapon
9   | Unready Weapon
10  | Casting
11  | Casting 2
12  | Readying Magic
13  | Unready Magic
14  | Knockdown
15  | Knocked Out
16  | Picking Probing
18  | Dying
19  | Dead
